Readings We will use a variety of text and manuscripts to assist with learning, plus additional online resources. The primary source material is from the two following textbooks with online resources:

  3. D.R. Olander and A.T. Motta, Light Water Reactor Materials: Volume I Fundamentals, ANS Science Publications, Illinois, 2017.
Outcomes
  • Provide an in-depth description and analysis of energetic particle interactions
  • Calculate the damage imparted to a crystal from energetic particle bombardments
  • Explain point-defect production and mobility as it relates to radiation damage
  • Identify key radiation damage produced 1D, 2D and 3D microstructures and the associated mechanisms as a function of dose, dose rate, and temperature
